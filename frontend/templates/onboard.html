What should A-Eye do with your photos?

A-Eye uses AI to understand your photos. Choose how you want it to work.

🔍
Catalogue Only
Analyse photos with AI. Descriptions, tags, and metadata stored in the database only. Your photo library stays completely untouched.
Best for: Exploring A-Eye risk-free, or when your library is managed by another tool
Your photos directory is mounted read-only. Only catalogue mode is available. You can change this later by remounting with write access, then adjust in Settings.
🏷
Enrich Metadata
Write AI-generated descriptions and tags to XMP sidecar files alongside your photos. No files are renamed.
Best for: Immich, Lightroom, and Photoprism users who want searchable AI metadata
✎
Full Processing
Rename photos with AI-generated descriptive filenames, write XMP metadata, or both. You choose exactly which actions to take.
Best for: Organising old photo libraries, bulk renaming camera dumps

Watch for new photos?

A-Eye can automatically detect new photos added to your library.

Manual Scan
